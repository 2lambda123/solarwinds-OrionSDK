    NCM.FirmwareOperations

    SolarWinds Information Service 2023.3 Schema Documentation

    Firmware operations data. Reading valid for Orion users with at least WebViewer NCM role. Creating, updating and deleting valid for Orion administrators with at least WebViewer NCM role.
